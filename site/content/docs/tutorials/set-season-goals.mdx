## Before you start

<Info>You'll need the athlete to already exist in your group, and a season to set the goal against. New to the building blocks? See [Concepts](/en/get-started/concepts) and the [Goals](/en/plan/goals) reference.</Info>

## What you'll do

You'll create a single goal for one athlete, break it into measurable key results, and leave it ready to track for the rest of the season.

By the end you'll have:

- A goal with a title, description, and its attributes (state, season, priority, supervisors, category) set.
- One or more key results, each with a start, current, and target value plus a target date.
- An optional methodology check run against your team's standards.
- A goal in a clear state, ready to track and review at season's end.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Goals and pick the athlete">
    Open **Goals** from the main menu. As a coach, pick a **group** and then the athlete you're setting the goal with. (As an athlete, your own goals open straight away.)
    {/* TODO(yarmill): step visual — the Goals list with a group and athlete selected. */}
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a new goal">
    Select **+** at the top of the goals list. A **New goal** appears, ready to edit. There's no Save button — changes apply immediately and are logged under **Activity**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the title, description, and attributes">
    Give the goal a title (for example, "Lift 150 kg on bench press") and a description of what success looks like and by when. Then set its attributes: **State** (start at *Not started*), **Season**, **Priority** (Low, Medium, or High), **Supervisors** (the coaches who follow it), and **Category** — a two-level label your team has configured.
    {/* TODO(yarmill): step visual — a new goal with title, description, and the attribute buttons. */}
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add key results">
    Type into the inline **Name the key result** row at the bottom of the table to add a row, then fill in **Start value**, **Current value**, and **Target value**, and select **Set date** to choose a target date. Add as many key results as the goal needs — they're the only way to break a goal down.
    {/* TODO(yarmill): step visual — the key results table with start, current, target values and a target date. */}
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run Document check (optional)">
    Select the **check icon** at the top right to open **Document check**, a methodology check from [Yollanda](/en/platform/yollanda). It flags what's still missing (red) or recommended (amber), with green checks for what's done, and links to your team's methodology. What counts as required differs between teams.
    {/* TODO(yarmill): step visual — the Document check panel with required and recommended items. */}
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the state and leave it ready to track">
    Move the goal's **State** to reflect where it stands — typically *On track* once it's set up — so it's ready to follow through the season.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## You're done

<Check>The goal is set and tracked all season — update each key result's current value manually as the athlete progresses, and move the state as the picture changes. It becomes the basis for the end-of-season review, and every change is logged automatically under **Activity** with who made it and when.</Check>
