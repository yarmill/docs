[Yollanda](/en/platform/yollanda) already sat one click away from the plan and the training log. Tabular was the gap — to ask about what was in front of you, you left the table first.

Now Yollanda opens straight from Tabular, from the same top-right icon you use elsewhere, with the table you are looking at already in context. Ask about the numbers on screen without breaking your flow.

### Improvements

- **Yollanda** — Her examples and prompts were refreshed to cover the newer modules and multi-athlete questions, so her suggestions match what you can actually ask today.
- **Load and readiness** — The load-and-readiness report now reflects illness and injury straight from the [medical module](/en/medical/medical-module) and shows the day's training focus from the [plan](/en/plan/plan) or the [training log](/en/reality/training-log), so the report reads true without duplicate entry.
- Plus a range of smaller improvements across the app.

### Fixes

- **Yollanda** — Report cost figures that looked inflated in chat now match the numbers in Analytics, dates inside generated links are formatted correctly, role counts in reports add up, and the AI Insights box no longer flickers while its description and interpretation are being written.
- Plus a number of smaller fixes and performance improvements.
