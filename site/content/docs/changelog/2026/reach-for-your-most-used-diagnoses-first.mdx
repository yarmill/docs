Logging an injury or illness meant scrolling the full diagnosis list every time, even though most of what you record day to day is a handful of the same conditions.

Now your most common diagnoses sit up front in the [Medical module](/en/medical/medical-module), so the everyday cases are one tap away. The complete list stays available alongside them for the rarer conditions you still need to find.

<Info>
  The shortlist of frequent diagnoses is configured per team, so each medical staff sees the conditions their setup uses most.
</Info>

## Yollanda keeps looking when the first search comes up empty

When you asked [Yollanda](/en/platform/yollanda) about something from earlier in the season, she searched a narrow recent window and sometimes returned nothing even though the answer was there.

Now she searches further back through your conversation history on her own when a first pass finds nothing, so questions about past sessions and notes are far more likely to land. She also reads activities synced from watches in your language, and no longer piles up duplicate notes when you ask about the same notes again in a conversation.

### Improvements

- **Trend analytics** — Sporttester data now feeds the trend views, so heart-rate and training data from those devices shows up alongside everything else you track over a season.
- **InBody** — Body-composition reports show more metrics and plot their data points in the right order, so segmental lean-mass readings are easier to read at a glance.
- **Swimming analysis** — The 800 m race report now breaks the swim down into 50 m segments with split times, matching the detail already available for longer distances.
- **Empty states** — Reports show clearer "no data" messages when a group or athlete has nothing to display, so an empty view explains itself instead of looking broken.
- Plus a range of smaller improvements across the app.

### Fixes

- **Daily readiness** — A false red warning no longer appears for athletes whose WHOOP recovery data is simply a few days old, so the readiness view reflects the real picture.
- **Reports** — Switching to a different report no longer silently changes which athlete you're viewing, so you stay on the person you selected.
- **Medical** — The last character you type in a note now saves reliably, and illnesses no longer show up mixed into the list of injuries.
- **Daily readiness** — Oura readiness now uses the correct colours after Oura's own redesign, so the scale reads as intended.
- Plus a number of smaller fixes and performance improvements.
