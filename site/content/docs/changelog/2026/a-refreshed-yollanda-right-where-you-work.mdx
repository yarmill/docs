[Yollanda](/en/platform/yollanda) gets a visual refresh this month, with a cleaner, calmer chat that's easier to read through a long conversation.

She's also wired into the [athlete profile](/en/platform/athlete-profile). Open her straight from a profile and she already has that athlete in context, so you can ask about the person in front of you without setting anything up first.

### Improvements

- **Medical** — A health issue can no longer be saved with a start date that falls after its end date, so the timeline an athlete's record builds on stays consistent and analytics read it correctly.
- **Wellness report** — The wellness report is now available to athletes, not just staff, so an athlete can follow their own recovery trend.
- **Report sharing** — Reports can now be opened up to a group or an individual athlete with the right filter applied, so you can give athletes access to their own data without exposing the rest of the squad.
- Plus a range of smaller improvements across the app.

### Fixes

- **Charts** — Category charts now show every value along the x-axis instead of clipping the last ones, and load and readiness KPIs read only from the actual side when you choose it.
- **Reports** — A training-shooting report no longer showed another athlete's name in its filter, so each athlete now sees only their own.
- **Copying plans** — Attachments now come along when you copy a plan from a group down to its athletes, instead of being left behind.
- **Command palette** — The command palette no longer hides behind dropdowns, and the Analytics entry is back in its menu.
- Plus a number of smaller fixes and performance improvements.
