Until now, [Yollanda](/en/platform/yollanda) answered questions one athlete at a time. To
get a read on a whole squad, you opened each athlete in turn and asked again.

Now Yollanda works at the squad level. Ask about a training squad and she reasons over
everyone at once — group overviews, the squad's plan, and side-by-side comparisons between
athletes. See who's trending up, who needs attention, and how the squad stacks up against
itself, all without leaving the group view.

## Open Yollanda wherever you're working

You no longer have to go looking for Yollanda to start a conversation. Open her directly
from an athlete's profile and from Tabular, and she keeps track of where you opened her —
so a question you ask from a profile already has that athlete in context.
