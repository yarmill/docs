Bring your VALD Performance testing into Yarmill. The integration pulls results from VALD's
devices — **ForceDecks**, **ForceFrame**, **NordBord** and **DynaMo** — so the jump,
strength and dynamometry tests your staff already run sit alongside the rest of an
athlete's data in the [reports](/en/platform/integrations).

This is built for the testing you do all season. Track countermovement jump (CMJ) trends
through the year, run your pre-season testing battery and compare it against last season,
and keep an eye on left/right asymmetries or strength deficits as they develop.

<Info>
  VALD Performance is connected per team. Each federation or club needs a custom setup on
  our side before data flows — get in touch to enable it for your instance.
</Info>

## Yollanda fits how each athlete trains

[Yollanda](/en/platform/yollanda) now reads each user's own settings before she answers.
She respects the heart-rate zones you've configured and the devices an athlete has
connected, so her analysis of load and recovery lines up with how that person actually
trains — not a generic default.

### Fixes

- **Athlete profiles** — Filtering in the Reporting view no longer surfaces records that shouldn't match, so a filtered list now shows exactly what you asked for.
- **Yollanda** — Her chat no longer hangs while loading Tabular data, she now validates the links she generates so they always point to the right module, and she reads WHOOP heart-rate data correctly. Logging of Yollanda interactions in Analytics is also working again after it had quietly stopped.
- Plus a number of smaller fixes and performance improvements.
