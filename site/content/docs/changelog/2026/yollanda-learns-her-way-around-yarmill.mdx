When you asked [Yollanda](/en/platform/yollanda) about something, she could explain it but couldn't take you there, and she didn't always know which parts of Yarmill a given athlete or team actually used.

Now she understands the app around her. She knows what each module is for, respects the modules you're allowed to see so she only works with what's available to you, and links straight into the relevant module, analysis or tabular so you can jump from an answer to the data behind it in one click.

## Tuned to how your team trains

Yollanda now factors in your instance's primary sport and focus, so her reasoning leans on what your team actually does rather than a generic default. She also reads the methodology guidance set up for your team, grounding her answers in your own coaching material.

<Info>
  A team's primary sport, available modules and methodology content are configured per instance, so Yollanda's awareness reflects your own setup.
</Info>

### Improvements

- **Typography** — Yarmill now renders in Inter, for cleaner, more consistent text across the app.
- **Focus states** — Keyboard navigation is more reliable: focus styling is consistent in the new design, more elements are reachable by Tab, and focused controls respond to Enter.
- **Wellness report** — On tablet and mobile, the dots for previous weeks no longer overlap, so past weeks stay readable on smaller screens.
- Plus a range of smaller improvements across the app.

### Fixes

- **Yollanda** — She no longer returns the occasional empty answer, recovers properly instead of failing outright after repeated retries, and her analysis stream is stable again.
- **Analytics** — An athlete's data now loads reliably even when it had previously appeared empty, and InBody filters no longer list users from outside the selected group.
- **Design** — Fixed a layout glitch in the goals and medical overview.
- Plus a number of smaller fixes and performance improvements.
