[Yollanda](/en/platform/yollanda) could already reason over training and recovery, but she didn't see the [Medical module](/en/medical/medical-module) — so questions about an athlete's injuries or availability fell outside what she could answer.

Now Yollanda reads the Medical module too. Ask about an athlete's injury history or current status and she factors it into her answer, alongside their training and recovery data.

## She follows your season structure

If your season runs on four-week mesocycles rather than calendar months, Yollanda now uses those numbers when she lays out an athlete's [training sheet](/en/plan/season-calendar) — so her reading of the year matches how you actually plan it.

<Info>
  Whether a season is split into 12 months or 13 four-week mesocycles is configured per instance, and Yollanda follows that setting.
</Info>

### Improvements

- **Tabular** — New rows now appear at the top of a table straight away, matching the newest-first order you already see, instead of dropping to the bottom until you refresh.
- **Admin invites** — You can now invite a teammate with the Admin role directly, without having to invite them as a coach first and change the role afterwards.
- **InBody** — The individual report now shows an athlete's full measurement history rather than trimming early results to the calendar range, so long-term body-composition trends stay complete.
- Plus a range of smaller improvements across the app.

### Fixes

- **Season calendar** — Events deleted from the calendar no longer reappear after a refresh, so removals now stick.
- **Analytics** — WHOOP data now shows up in trend analysis, not just in the training-load report, so athletes on a WHOOP band get a complete trend view.
- **Yollanda** — Her answers now include days an athlete left blank in the diary instead of skipping them, she picks up fresh entries right after you add them rather than serving stale cached data, and she no longer stalls or errors on athletes with little or no data. Date formatting, text formatting in interpretations, and her reading of right-side column descriptions are all corrected too.
- Plus a number of smaller fixes and performance improvements.
