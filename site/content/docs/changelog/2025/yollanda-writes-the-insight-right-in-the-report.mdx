Reading a report well takes practice. The numbers are there, but knowing what to make of them is its own skill.

Now [Yollanda](/en/platform/yollanda) reads the report for you and writes a short interpretation right alongside it. Open a report and her AI Insights spell out what the data is saying, so the takeaway is there without you having to piece it together yourself.

### Improvements

- **Weekly dashboard** — The watch-data tooltip now shows the number of workouts, so you can see how much activity sits behind the week's totals at a glance.
- Plus a range of smaller improvements across the app.

### Fixes

- **Yollanda** — She no longer exposes technical implementation details in her answers, her AI Insights bullets are formatted correctly, and the AI Insights icon no longer stays stuck blue in Chrome.
- Plus a number of smaller fixes and performance improvements.
