Until now, [Yollanda](/en/platform/yollanda) reasoned over what was logged in the training diary. The data flowing in from athletes' watches and bands sat outside her reach, so questions about a recorded session or last night's recovery went unanswered.

Now Yollanda reads that data too. She picks up whole activities synced from a watch, with their sport, duration and heart-rate zones, and she pulls in health metrics like HRV, resting heart rate and sleep. Ask how an athlete recovered after a hard block or how their sleep tracked through a camp, and her answer draws on the same numbers their device recorded.

Yollanda treats wearable data and diary entries as two separate sources of the "same" measurement, so she won't double-count a session that exists in both places. She also knows which provider each athlete is connected to, so she can tell whose numbers come from where.

## A coach view over Oura data

Athletes on Oura now feed a dedicated coach report. See sleep and recovery trends from the ring across your group in one place, alongside the rest of an athlete's data.

### Improvements

- **Yollanda** — Tables and charts in her chat now render in a monospace layout, so columns line up and data is easier to read at a glance.
- **Biochemistry** — Positive changes between two CASRI lab measurements now carry a `+` sign, matching the `-` already shown for decreases, so the direction of every change reads clearly.
- **Race analysis** — In swimming you can now compare across different disciplines, for example a relay leg against a solo race, instead of being limited to matching events.
- Plus a range of smaller improvements across the app.

### Fixes

- **Daily readiness** — WHOOP Recovery no longer appears for athletes who don't have a WHOOP connected, so the team readiness view only shows data that actually exists.
- **Yollanda** — Links she generates now work reliably, including links with underscores, and her analysis no longer stalls intermittently.
- **Rich text** — Notes no longer show stray HTML characters or an extra blank line, and bullet formatting renders correctly.
- **Files** — A file uploaded on the left side now appears immediately, without needing a page refresh.
- Plus a number of smaller fixes and performance improvements.
