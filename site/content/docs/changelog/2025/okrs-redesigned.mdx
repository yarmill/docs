Setting and reviewing season [goals](/en/plan/goals) had grown slow and cramped, and there was no real way to work with them on a phone.

Now OKRs have a fresh design throughout. Objectives and key results are clearer to scan, filtering is faster to narrow down to the goals you care about, and loading and saving are quicker so editing a goal no longer means waiting. The view now works properly on mobile, so you can check in on progress between sessions.

## Export goals for the right season

Exporting goals used to print whichever season happened to be selected, with no way to choose. Now the export lets you pick the season you want, so the printed goals always match what you meant to share.

You can also jump straight from a goal to its methodology guidance, and coaches and admins are now marked distinctly in the side menu so you can tell at a glance who is who.

### Improvements

- **Recovery analytics** — The recovery report now covers more of the picture, giving you a fuller read on how athletes are bouncing back.
- **Report icons** — More reports now use the refreshed icon set, so the look stays consistent as you move between them.
- Plus a range of smaller improvements across the app.

### Fixes

- **Yollanda** — [Yollanda](/en/platform/yollanda) now addresses you directly rather than in the third person, and typing to her on mobile web works smoothly.
- **OKRs** — Clicking outside a dropdown now closes it cleanly, logos no longer appear distorted in the redesigned view, and a cancelled key result correctly shows as closed in its drawer.
- **Reports** — Whole numbers and chart labels now display correctly instead of being cut off or misplaced, and the weekly dashboard uses a distinct icon for a logged session that differs from the plan.
- Plus a number of smaller fixes and performance improvements.
