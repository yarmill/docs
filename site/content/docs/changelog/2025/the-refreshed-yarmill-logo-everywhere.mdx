A few corners of the product still carried the old Yarmill logo — in PDF exports, on the iOS and Android apps, and in the mobile widget and announcements.

Now the current logo appears consistently across all of them, so the app and everything it produces look like one Yarmill.

### Improvements

- **Analytics** — Trend charts now show the Y axis, its labels, and matching gridlines by default, so a trend is easier to read at a glance without extra setup.
- Plus a range of smaller improvements across the app.

### Fixes

- **Training log** — Workouts that already existed now show up immediately when a coach opens an athlete's reality view, instead of appearing only after a manual refresh.
- **Yollanda** — Season selection now keys off the date alone, so her evaluation no longer slips around the time of day as a season ends.
- Plus a number of smaller fixes and performance improvements.
