The rebuilt [Yollanda](/en/platform/yollanda) has been running with a handful of teams while we put her through real season use. She's ready for everyone now.

Yollanda is switched on across nearly every instance, with an in-app announcement that shows what she can do, how she looks, and where to find her. Ask her about an athlete's load, recovery, or plan and get a straight answer without leaving the view you're in.

<Info>
  Yollanda is enabled per team. A few instances without both plan and reality data stay out of scope for now — get in touch if you'd like her turned on.
</Info>

## Insights that read your charts for you

A chart shows the trend, but reading it still takes a trained eye. Yollanda now sits on your analytics charts: tap the insights button on a graph and she explains what's actually visible in it — the rise, the dip, the outlier week — in plain language right next to the data.

### Improvements

- **Yollanda** — She's less chatty on small talk now. Greet her and she greets you back and asks how she can help, instead of launching into an unprompted analysis.
- **Yollanda** — Every answer in the Yollanda module and in [Analytics](/en/analytics/analytics) insights now carries a clear note that it's AI-generated and can be wrong, so it's obvious when to double-check a number.
- Plus a range of smaller improvements across the app.

### Fixes

- **Files** — Files from a previous user no longer linger in an athlete's [profile records](/en/platform/athlete-profile) when the next person opens it, so each athlete sees only their own attachments.
- **Files** — Adding attachments to a record is reliable again: newly uploaded files now appear and stay put instead of flashing up and vanishing.
- **Analytics** — Decimal values entered in records no longer come back padded with trailing zeros after a refresh, so 179 stays 179.
- **Analytics** — Trend analysis decomposes heart-rate zones correctly again, and biochemistry markers keep one consistent colour between the detail and trend charts.
- **Analytics** — Yollanda's chart insights now render stacked bar charts correctly, and the insights icon stays inside the chart area instead of drifting outside it.
- **Mobile** — The Yollanda icon is visible again on mobile, the app no longer crashes when syncing a snowboarding activity from Apple Watch, and the in-app announcement opens at the top instead of scrolled halfway down.
- **Results** — An athlete's name now prints on the results-trend PDF instead of being left blank.
- Plus a number of smaller fixes and performance improvements.
