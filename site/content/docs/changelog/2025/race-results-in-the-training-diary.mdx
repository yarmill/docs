A race result used to live separately from the day it belonged to. Now an IBU race summary shows up right in the left side of the [training log](/en/reality/training-log), alongside the watch and health data for that day.

Each summary carries what you'd want at a glance: the event type, the venue, the discipline and distance, the shooting line, and the finishing place. It gives a race day the same at-a-glance card the rest of the day already has.

<Info>
  Race summaries come from the IBU data feed and apply to biathlon instances. Availability depends on your team's setup.
</Info>

### Improvements

- **Analytics** — Reports stay on the same athlete as you move between load-and-readiness dashboards, recovery analytics label their average lines more clearly, and an unfilled wellness questionnaire over the last seven days is now shown for what it is.
- **Charts** — Bar-chart data labels sit in a clearer position, and richtext business formats can set their own text colour.
- Plus a range of smaller improvements across the app.

### Fixes

- **Analytics** — Players no longer drop out of the filter in the GPS report, nested visuals show their labels, and team daily readiness correctly reflects whether the wellbeing questionnaire was filled in.
- **Polar** — Connecting Yarmill to Polar Flow no longer errors out.
- **Charts** — Horizontal column charts now show negative values and render outside-positioned labels.
- Plus a number of smaller fixes and performance improvements.
