We've been refining the [medical module](/en/medical/medical-module) through the season, and this drop tidies up the things you touch every day. The naming is now consistent end to end: what you track is **health issues**, the same word in the record, the activity log and the reports.

The activity log reads more cleanly too. Related changes are grouped together the way goals already are, so a string of edits to one injury shows as one block instead of a wall of separate lines. The drawer's empty state, the action icons and the date picker all got a pass, and the whole module works properly on a phone.

## Yollanda answers from fresher data, and your own methodology

[Yollanda](/en/platform/yollanda) used to reason over diary data that was recalculated once a night, so the training an athlete logged this morning wasn't there yet when you asked. Now, when you open her, she pulls in anything edited in roughly the last day and merges it in, so her answer reflects what just happened.

She also follows your team's own coaching guidance. Instance-specific methodical notes can be fed into her guidelines, so her answers speak your program's language rather than a generic default.

<Info>
  The methodical guidance Yollanda draws on is configured per team. Talk to us about adding your program's notes.
</Info>

### Improvements

- **Garmin** — Workouts now carry the name of the device that recorded them, and all Garmin data is clearly marked as coming from Garmin, so you can see at a glance where a number originated.
- **Athlete profiles** — Stepped questionnaires can show the description text for an attribute or question, and sharing a link straight to a questionnaire is cleaner.
- **Text fields** — You can click anywhere in a text area to start typing, not just on the first line.
- Plus a range of smaller improvements across the app.

### Fixes

- **Yollanda** — She no longer formats dates as a numbered list, refers to an athlete in the second person when they ask about themselves, and gets time values in minutes right. Stray invalid links and over-eager use of module guidelines on general questions are fixed too.
- **Analytics** — Reports keep loading when you switch dashboards while viewing as an athlete, InBody and movement time-charts label their X-axis correctly, and the weekly micro-cycle team overview shows the right week numbers across a season boundary.
- **Medical** — You can now confirm circumstances with Enter, and adding a new injury through the plus button works again.
- Plus a number of smaller fixes and performance improvements.
