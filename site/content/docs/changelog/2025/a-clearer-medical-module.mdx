Recording an injury or illness in the [Medical module](/en/medical/medical-module) asked for more detail than most staff could give, and the diagnosis catalogue was English-only. The result was half-filled records that were hard to read back later.

Now the module is simpler and clearer to fill in. The diagnosis catalogue is available in Czech alongside English, the diagnosis section shows the affected side right where you record an injury, and the confusing injury-onset list is gone. When a health issue closes, it's marked **Full training** automatically, so an athlete's status stays current without a manual step.

<Info>
  The set of injury circumstances is configured per team. Football clubs, for example, can now log mechanism of injury, playing surface and severity from purpose-built lists.
</Info>

### Improvements

- The Medical overview now gives each record room to breathe, so statuses, names and expected return dates stay readable instead of crowding together.
- **Headings** — Table, report and dashboard titles now use sentence case consistently across the app.
- Plus a range of smaller improvements across the app.

### Fixes

- **Medical module** — Diagnoses and comments now save reliably and survive a refresh, and on mobile the cross button for clearing a date or circumstance works again.
- Plus a number of smaller fixes and performance improvements.
