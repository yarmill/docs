You make the instance ready: people in, groups built, permissions right. After that,
coaches and athletes follow their own paths.

## First-time setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create groups">
    In **Settings → Groups**, select **+ New group** for each squad your coaches plan
    for.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add members">
    Open a group and add its members. Each person gets a **role** — Athlete, Coach, or
    Admin — and coaches get their **permissions** (for example write access).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send invitations">
    Invited people get an email with a join link and the team URL; their membership
    dates are tracked on the member list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Day to day

Access questions land on you: "I can't see the module/athlete" is usually a permission
or configuration, fixed in **Settings → Groups**. For instance-level changes — new
fields, modules, analytics — contact `hello@yarmill.com`.
