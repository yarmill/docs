## Your first week

<Steps>
  <Step title="Plan on the group">
    In **Plan**, select **Entire Group** and write the week — text on the left, numbers
    on the right. This is your private sandbox; athletes see nothing yet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish with Copy plan">
    Select **Copy plan**, choose the days and athletes, and confirm. Now they see it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Individualize">
    Select a single athlete and adjust their week — visible to them immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Reality">
    As the week runs, open **Reality** per athlete to see diaries, RPE, and watch data
    against your plan.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the morning readiness">
    In **Analytics → Team daily readiness**, see who's green before training starts.
  </Step>
</Steps>
