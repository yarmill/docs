<Info>The dimensions and their scales are **configured per team** — the number of items, the scale length, and each label vary. The example below is one instance's set: **Sleep**, **Stress**, **Mood**, **Soreness**, **Fatigue**, plus a free-text note.</Info>

On its own each entry is small. Across a squad and across weeks it becomes the team's readiness picture, turning subjective state into trended data before the numbers from devices catch up.

## Fill it in

Athletes step through the questionnaire as a short wizard — **one question per step**, with large tappable options and **Back** / **Continue** to move between them.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the questionnaire">
    From your [Training Log](/en/reality/training-log), open the day and choose **wellness
    questionnaire** from the day card's **⋯** (more) menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the date">
    The first step is the **date** the record is for — it defaults to today.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Rate each dimension">
    Step through each dimension in turn — in this instance **Sleep**, **Stress**, **Mood**,
    **Soreness**, then **Fatigue**. Each step shows a plain-language question and a row of
    options, each a **number** and a label (for example *1 Excellent* … *5 Very bad sleep*).
    Tap your answer and **Continue**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a note">
    The final step is a free-text **note to the coach** — use it when something's off ("slept
    badly, travel") so it gets read alongside the numbers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Submitting saves the day's record. The questionnaire is **not** auto-saved — closing it
    part-way prompts to confirm, and the record is created only when you finish.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>Fill it in each morning, before training. Readiness data is only useful before the session — filled in after, it's just history.</Tip>

## One record per day

There's a single record per day per athlete. Re-opening the same day edits that record rather than adding a second one.

Each record is kept in a per-athlete table — one row per day, with a column for each dimension (here **Date · Sleep · Stress · Mood · Soreness · Fatigue · Notes**) and every header filterable. From the table you can **edit** or **delete** any day's record.

## For coaches

Select an athlete from the group panel to read their records. A coach can also fill a record **on an athlete's behalf** — **New record** opens the same wizard — and edit or delete any row from the athlete's table.

For the group view, head to [Analytics](/en/analytics/analytics): the **Wellness** analysis trends each metric over four weeks, and **Team daily readiness** combines the day's self-report with device recovery data and medical status for the morning overview.

## How it connects

- The day's self-report feeds the [Wellness analysis and Team daily
  readiness](/en/analytics/analytics) — the single morning "who's ready" view.
- It complements the same day's [Training Log](/en/reality/training-log), which is also where
  athletes reach the questionnaire.
- It sits alongside the other readiness inputs — device recovery from
  [Integrations](/en/platform/integrations) and training-limitation status from the
  [Medical module](/en/medical/medical-module).
