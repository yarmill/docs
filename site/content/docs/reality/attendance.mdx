<Info>Attendance is an **optional module, enabled per team** — aimed at groups that don't self-log. Whether it appears at all is configured for your instance.</Info>

Attendance is a coach-side register of who actually showed up, totalled into **present / missed / %** numbers you can report. It's built for groups whose athletes don't keep their own diary — sport schools and youth squads — and it doubles as the source for participation reporting to schools and national agencies. The morning register, the state set, and the season percentages are the same everywhere.

## Take attendance

Open **Attendance** in the **Week** view. The grid has an **Entire Group** summary row at the top and one row per athlete, with a column for each day **Mon–Sun** and totals on the right.

Each day column has an **Edit** link beneath its date. Select it to put that day into edit mode — every athlete's cell shows four state buttons:

<ParamField path="Present">A green circle-check — the athlete was at training.</ParamField>
<ParamField path="Free">A coffee cup — a rest day or day off. This is the default state.</ParamField>
<ParamField path="Excused">A circle-minus — a notified, agreed absence.</ParamField>
<ParamField path="Not excused">A circle-cross — an unexplained no-show.</ParamField>

**Set the whole group at once** with the buttons on the **Entire Group** row — pressing **Present** there marks everyone present for that day, then you adjust the exceptions. Select **Close** on the column header when you're done.

The right-hand columns keep a running **Total present**, **Total missed**, and attendance **%** for each athlete and for the group.

## See the season picture

Switch from **Week** to **Season** for the long run: the columns become the months **Jan–Dec** with the same **present / missed / %** totals, and a year selector replaces the week navigator. Read this at period or season end to see each athlete's participation rate.

## Fill Reality from attendance

For groups where one coach logs everything, **Copy plan** (top right) writes the planned week into **Reality** — but only for athletes marked **present** that day. The session shows up in their diaries without anyone self-logging.

<Warning>Attendance-based copy writes a whole day into athletes' diaries. Don't use it for athletes who keep their own [Reality](/en/reality/training-log) — it would overwrite their entries.</Warning>

## Export participation

The **export** control (top right) downloads the register for handing to a school or agency — as a **PDF**, or as a **monthly Excel (XLSX)** file.

## How it connects

- **Copy plan** fills the [Training Log](/en/reality/training-log) for present athletes — the
  main flow out of Attendance.
- The week being copied comes from the group's [Plan](/en/plan/plan).
- Per-period and per-season **%** are the numbers you export for participation reporting.
