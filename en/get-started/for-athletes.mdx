As an athlete you do three things in Yarmill: see your plan, log what you actually did,
and let your devices fill in the rest. You see only your own data — your goals, your
diary, your analytics.

**For:** athletes · **Where:** Mobile app, PWA, and web

## Get set up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Use your email, password, and your team URL (it's in your confirmation email).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill your profile">
    Complete your **Athlete profile** — your team uses it for contacts, sizes, and IDs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your watch">
    In **Settings → Applications & devices**, connect Garmin, Polar, Suunto, WHOOP,
    Oura, or Dexcom — Apple Watch connects from the iOS app.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Every day

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check the plan">
    Today's training, with anything your coach attached.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill the wellness questionnaire">
    A minute each morning — it's how your coach knows you're ready (or not).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log your training the same day">
    In **Reality**, describe the session in your own words and fill in your numbers.
    If your team enables it, **import plan** pre-fills the day for editing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>Same-day logging is the habit that makes everything else work — entries written
a week later are guesses.</Tip>
