Attendance tracks who showed up — morning and afternoon — and totals it into
participation numbers you can report. It's built for groups whose athletes don't keep
their own diary, like sport schools and youth squads.

**For:** coaches · **Where:** Web app

## Take attendance

Open **Attendance** and pick the week. Each athlete has a cell per day — select **Edit**
on a day to mark who was present, off, excused, or unexcused. The right-hand columns
total **present**, **missed**, and the attendance **%**; switch the view from **Week**
to **Season** for the long-run picture.

## Fill Reality from attendance

**Copy plan** from the Attendance screen fills Reality only for athletes marked
**present** that day — useful when one coach logs for a whole young group.

<Warning>Attendance-based copy writes into athletes' diaries for the whole day. Don't
use it for athletes who log their own Reality — it would overwrite their entries.</Warning>
