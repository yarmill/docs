The wellness questionnaire is a short morning self-report. A minute a day from each
athlete adds up to the team's readiness picture: trends in the
[Wellness analysis](/en/analytics/analytics) and the morning
[Team daily readiness](/en/analytics/analytics) overview.

**For:** athletes (fill in), coaches (review) · **Where:** Web app and mobile

<Info>The questions are configured per team. A typical set: **Sleep**, **Stress**,
**Mood**, **Soreness**, and **Fatigue**, each on a short labeled scale (for example
*1 – Excellent* to *5*), plus an optional note to the coach.</Info>

## Fill it in

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the questionnaire">
    Select **Wellness questionnaire**, or open it from the day's **⋯** menu in your
    Training Log.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Answer the questions">
    Rate each item on its scale and add a **note** if there's something your coach
    should know.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Done — it's saved">
    Each day is one record; you can edit or delete a record from the list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>Fill it in each morning before training — readiness data is only useful before
the session, not after.</Tip>

## For coaches

Select an athlete to see their records, or head to **Analytics** for the group view:
the **Wellness** analysis trends each metric over four weeks, and **Team daily
readiness** combines wellness with device recovery data and medical status for the
morning overview.
