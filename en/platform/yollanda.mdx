Yollanda is Yarmill's AI layer. Unlike a generic chatbot, she has the training data in
front of her — so "compare April and May," "when did the left shoulder last come up,"
or "how do I publish a plan" all get answers grounded in your Yarmill.

**For:** everyone (you see only data you have access to) · **Where:** Web app

## Ask Yollanda

Select the **Yollanda icon** in the header to open the chat, and ask in your own
language — she answers in the language you ask. Two kinds of questions work well:

- **About the data**: summaries, comparisons, finding things in the diary
  ("summary of last season," "bench-press kilos from the last gym session").
- **About Yarmill**: where to click and how workflows work.

Athletes can ask only about their own data; coaches about any athlete they can access.

<Info>Yollanda is in **beta** — as the chat itself puts it, "even Yollanda sometimes
gropes in the fog." Verify important information before acting on it.</Info>

## Insights on charts

In Analytics, charts and summary cards carry a small Yollanda icon. Select it for a
plain-language description of the chart and an interpretation of what it means.
Insights are computed overnight, so they appear the day after a chart is enabled.

## What she can't do

Yollanda answers; she doesn't change your instance, log entries for you, or pass
messages to the Yarmill team. For configuration changes or feedback, talk to your
administrator or write to `hello@yarmill.com`.
