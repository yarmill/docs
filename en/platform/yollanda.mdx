Yollanda is Yarmill's conversational AI assistant. Unlike a generic chatbot, she has your
training data in front of her — so an answer is drawn from your Yarmill, scoped to what you
can already see, rather than from the open internet.

**For:** coaches and athletes · **Where:** Web app

<Info>Yollanda is in **beta**. As the chat itself puts it, "even Yollanda sometimes gropes in
the fog" — verify important information before acting on it.</Info>

## Ask Yollanda

Select the **blush Yollanda icon** in the header to open the chat. It opens full-screen with a
**"Just ask Yollanda"** input at the bottom; the empty state introduces her ("I am one big
ear.") alongside the reminder to verify what she tells you. Ask questions about your training
data in natural language, in whatever language you prefer — she answers in the language you
ask. Two kinds of questions work well:

- **About the data**: summaries, comparisons, and finding things in the diary
  ("summary of last season," "bench-press kilos from the last gym session").
- **About Yarmill**: where to click and how workflows work ("how do I publish a plan").

Yollanda only ever sees the data you already have access to — she never widens your
permissions. Athletes can ask about their own data; coaches about any athlete they can access.

## Insights on charts

In [Analytics](/en/analytics/analytics), charts and summary cards carry a small Yollanda icon.
Select it for a plain-language description of the chart and an interpretation of what it means.
Insights are computed overnight, so they appear the day after a chart is enabled, and not every
chart carries one.

## What she can't do

Yollanda answers; she doesn't change your instance, log entries for you, or pass messages to
the Yarmill team. For configuration changes or feedback, talk to your administrator or write to
`hello@yarmill.com`.

## How it connects

- Data answers are grounded in your [Training Log](/en/reality/training-log) and
  [Plan](/en/plan/plan) — the diary and plan made queryable in your own words.
- Chart **Insights** read your [Analytics](/en/analytics/analytics) charts and turn them into
  plain language.
- The **Document check** in [Goals](/en/plan/goals) is another Yollanda surface — it scores a
  goal against your team's methodology.
