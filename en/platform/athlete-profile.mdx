The Athlete profile is the central card for each person — the place that replaces the
scattered spreadsheets of contacts, equipment sizes, and IDs.

**For:** coaches, athletes (own card) · **Where:** Web app

<Info>Beyond **Personal info**, the sections and fields are configured per team — for
example sports info, equipment sizes, emergency contacts, documents, or external IDs
that link federation databases.</Info>

## The card

Open **Athlete profiles** and select an athlete. **Personal info** is always there —
**First name**, **Last name**, **Email**, **Date of birth**, **Gender**, **Status**,
and **Notes** — each field edited in place with the pencil. Your instance's additional
sections follow the same pattern.

Athletes can edit the fields their team marks as athlete-editable (shoe sizes change;
passports expire) — keeping the card current is a shared job.

<Tip>Filling the profile is the first thing to do in a new account, together with
[connecting your devices](/en/platform/integrations).</Tip>
