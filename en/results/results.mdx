Results keeps the outcomes — races, matches, tests — in structured tables per athlete,
so performance over time is data, not memory.

**For:** coaches, athletes · **Where:** Web app

<Info>Results tables are configured per team and sport. The columns below are one
team's example; yours will match your sport — and some sports import results
automatically from federation databases (for example IBU or FIS), so you may not need
to enter races at all.</Info>

## Record a result

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select the athlete">
    Open **Results** and choose an athlete — results are per person.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the result">
    Select **New result** and fill in the fields — for example **Date**, **Location**,
    **Weather**, **Discipline**, **Rank**, and a **Note**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Use the column filters to find results by date, discipline, or place.

## Testing & Diagnostics

Teams that run structured testing — strength, lactate, body composition, sport-specific
protocols — record them in configurable test forms of the same kind, which then feed
test reports in Analytics. If your team uses them, you'll find them alongside Results.
