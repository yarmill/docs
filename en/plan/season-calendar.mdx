The Season Calendar (also called the **Planner**) holds the season's fixed points —
training camps, competitions, testing days, vacations — on one year-long wall. Events
you add here flow into the [Plan](/en/plan/plan) views on their dates, so the week always
carries its context. Athletes can add their own events too, which makes the calendar a
coach–athlete communication channel.

**For:** coaches, athletes · **Where:** Web app

<Info>The Planner is on Yarmill's new design and is being refreshed — exact controls and
screens may differ from this guide, and the Plan and Planner are slated to merge over
time. The mechanics below are stable.</Info>

## Event types

Each event has a type, so the calendar reads at a glance:

- **Training camp**
- **Competition / race**
- **Training**
- **Testing**
- **Vacation / time off**
- **Other**

## Add an event

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Season Calendar">
    Open **Planner** (Season Calendar) from the main menu and find the date.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the event">
    Add an event and choose its **type**, then set the **title** and **start/end** dates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the location and participants">
    Add a **location** (searchable on the map) and choose **participants** — the whole
    group or selected athletes. You can target a sub-group, for example a national vs. a
    development squad.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add notes">
    Anything the event should carry — logistics, agenda, links.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>Athletes can add their own events too — a family holiday, say — so their coach
sees it on the same calendar.</Tip>

## How events reach the Plan

Events appear automatically in the [Plan](/en/plan/plan) views on their dates and are
**locked there** — edit them back in the Season Calendar, not in the Plan. An admin can
also lock an event so it can't be changed.
