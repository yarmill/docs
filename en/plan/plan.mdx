The Plan is where you decide what training should happen — from the shape of the whole
season down to Tuesday morning's session — and publish it to your athletes when it's
ready.

**For:** coaches · **Where:** Web app

<Info>Yarmill is configured for each team, so the training phases, activities, and
data rows you see may differ from this guide. The mechanics work the same way.</Info>

## The four views

Plan gives you the same season at four zoom levels, switched at the top of the page:

- **Week** — the working view: each day with its **left (text) side** (for example
  *daily goal*, *morning*, *afternoon*) and the **right (data) side** grid of numbers
  for the week, with auto-calculated totals.
- **Goals** — four weeks side by side, for comparing the same days across a mesocycle.
- **Top-down** — the whole season in three columns: **Mesocycles**, **Microcycles
  (Weeks)**, and **Days**. Give each a theme or main goal; start here at season start.
- **Annual plan** — the season-start intention, entered by mesocycle/month. This is
  what the Season review later compares your plan and reality against.

## Your sandbox, and publishing

Planning on the **Entire Group** is your private sandbox — athletes see nothing until
you publish. That's deliberate: sketch freely, then release the week when it's ready.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Plan the week on the group">
    Select **Entire Group**, open the week, and write the days — text on the left,
    numbers on the right. Everything auto-saves.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Copy plan">
    Choose **Copy plan** (top right). A dialog opens.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose what and who">
    Pick the destination — **athletes in group**, **other group**, or **another week** —
    then select the **source days** to include (hold back unfinished days, for example
    Friday–Sunday), whether to **copy the weekly goal**, and which **athletes** receive
    it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Select **Copy plan** in the dialog. Your athletes now see the week as planned.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>Publish early, then individualize. Selecting one athlete and editing their plan
changes only their copy — and is visible to them immediately, no publishing needed.</Tip>

## Individualize after publishing

Select a single athlete in the left list and edit their week — tailor load, swap
activities, adjust days. Only their plan changes. From an individual you can also copy
a plan back up to the group or to specific teammates with **Copy plan**.

## Events from the Season Calendar

Camps, races, and testing days planned in the [Season Calendar](/en/plan/season-calendar)
appear automatically in the Plan views on their dates, so the week always carries its
context.
